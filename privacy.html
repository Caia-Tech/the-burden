Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 30, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how The Burden ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your data when you visit theburden.org. By accessing this site, you consent to monitoring for security purposes.

Data Collection

We collect standard analytics data including:

  • IP addresses
  • Visit timestamps
  • Page interactions
  • Browser and device information
  • Referral sources

This data is collected for security and improvement purposes.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Cloudflare - For content delivery, security, and DDoS protection
  • Google Analytics - For understanding site usage patterns
  • GitHub - For code repository hosting

These services may collect additional data according to their own privacy policies.

Data Retention

Security logs are retained indefinitely for legal compliance. We preserve all access logs for potential legal proceedings. Other analytics data may be retained for up to 2 years.

Cookies

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Analytics Cookies - To understand how visitors use our site
  • Security Cookies - To protect against malicious activity
  • Preference Cookies - To remember your theme and reading progress

User Rights

Under GDPR and similar regulations, you have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (except security logs required for legal compliance)
  • Object to data processing
  • Data portability

To exercise these rights, contact: owner@caiatech.com

Security

We employ industry-standard security measures including Cloudflare protection. Security incidents are logged and investigated. We cooperate with legal authorities when required by law.

Legal Compliance

We preserve all access logs for potential legal proceedings. By accessing this site, you acknowledge that your access may be monitored and logged for security and legal purposes.

Updates

This policy was updated on June 30, 2025, demonstrating our commitment to active maintenance and transparency. We may update this policy as needed and will note the date of changes.
